# Campfire Board — v1

16 spaces in a loop. Players start at **Campfire** (space 0). Movement is clockwise.

The shipped board artwork lives at [`assets/print/pdf/board.pdf`](../../assets/print/pdf/board.pdf). Tile faces use a slightly compressed copy of the effects below to fit at table-legible sizes; both forms are normative and equivalent. The compressed wording is canonical for the tile face; the full wording is canonical for the rules text.

| # | Space | Tile face (printed on board) | Full effect |
|---|-------|------------------------------|-------------|
| 0 | **Campfire** | Safe. +1 coin if you pass through. | Safe space. Collect 1 coin if you pass through (like "Go"). Start position. |
| 1 | **Workshop** | Pay 2c -> +1 Upgrade. | Pay 2 coins to gain 1 Upgrade. |
| 2 | **Market** | Buy or sell 1 resource at posted price. | Buy or sell 1 resource at the current Market Price. |
| 3 | **Rumor Mill** | Draw an Event card. | Draw an Event card and resolve it immediately. |
| 4 | **Trade Dock** | Propose a trade with any player. | You may propose a trade with any other player (coins, vouchers, or resources). |
| 5 | **Festival** | Donate 1c -> +1 Rep. Optional. | All players may donate 1 coin to gain +1 Reputation. Optional. |
| 6 | **Trouble** | Lose 1c OR lose 1 Rep. Your choice. | Lose 1 coin OR lose 1 Reputation. Your choice. |
| 7 | **Help Desk** | Give 1c to another. Both gain +1 Rep. | Pick another player: give them 1 coin, you both gain +1 Reputation. |
| 8 | **Mint** | +2 coins from the bank. | Gain 2 coins from the bank. |
| 9 | **Rumor Mill** | Draw an Event card. | Draw an Event card and resolve it immediately. |
| 10 | **Builder** | Pay 3c -> +1 Upgrade. Need Rep >= 3. | Pay 3 coins to gain 1 Upgrade. Requires Reputation >= 3. |
| 11 | **Faucet** | +1 coin from the bank. | Gain 1 coin from the bank. |
| 12 | **Trade Dock** | Propose a trade with any player. | You may propose a trade with any other player. |
| 13 | **Taxman** | Pay 1c. Can't? Lose 1 Rep. | Pay 1 coin to the bank. If you can't pay, lose 1 Reputation instead. |
| 14 | **Commons** | All vote. Majority yes -> everyone +1c. | All players vote: if majority agrees, everyone gains 1 coin. Otherwise nothing. |
| 15 | **Crossroads** | Draw a Deal card. Accept or pass. | Draw a Deal card. You may accept or pass. |

## Board notes

- Two **Rumor Mill** spaces (3 and 9) ensure Events come up frequently.
- Two **Trade Dock** spaces (4 and 12) encourage player interaction.
- **Workshop** (cheap upgrade) vs **Builder** (expensive upgrade + rep gate) creates a progression feel.
- **Commons** is the first taste of collective action / governance.
- **Crossroads** introduces Deals — optional commitments with future payoff.
