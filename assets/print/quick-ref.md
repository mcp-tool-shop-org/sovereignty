# Quick Reference — Sovereignty: Campfire

## Board Spaces (0-15, clockwise)

| # | Space | What happens |
|---|-------|-------------|
| 0 | Campfire | +1 coin (safe). Also +1 when passing through. |
| 1 | Workshop | Pay 2 coins -> +1 Upgrade |
| 2 | Market | Buy or sell 1 resource at posted price |
| 3 | Rumor Mill | Draw an Event card |
| 4 | Trade Dock | Propose a trade with any player |
| 5 | Festival | Donate 1 coin -> +1 Rep |
| 6 | Trouble | Lose 1 coin OR lose 1 Rep |
| 7 | Help Desk | Give 1 coin to another player, both +1 Rep |
| 8 | Mint | +2 coins |
| 9 | Rumor Mill | Draw an Event card |
| 10 | Builder | Pay 3 coins -> +1 Upgrade (need Rep >= 3) |
| 11 | Faucet | +1 coin |
| 12 | Trade Dock | Propose a trade with any player |
| 13 | Taxman | Pay 1 coin. Can't? Lose 1 Rep. |
| 14 | Commons | Vote: if majority agrees, everyone +1 coin |
| 15 | Crossroads | Draw a Deal card. Accept or pass. |

## Turn Order

1. Roll d6, move clockwise
2. Do what the space says
3. Optional: propose one trade
4. End turn

## Promises

- Once per round: say "I promise..." out loud
- Keep it: **+1 Rep**
- Break it: **-2 Rep**
- The table decides

## The Apology

- Once per game
- Pay 1 coin to who you wronged
- Regain +1 Rep

## Winning

| Goal | Condition |
|------|-----------|
| Prosperity | 20+ coins |
| Beloved | 10 reputation |
| Builder | 4+ upgrades |

**After 15 rounds:** `(Coins / 2) + Rep + (Upgrades * 3)` = highest wins

## Voucher Rules

- Need Rep >= 2 to issue
- Rep >= 5: vouchers pay face value +1 (trusted)
- Miss the deadline: automatic default, lose Rep
