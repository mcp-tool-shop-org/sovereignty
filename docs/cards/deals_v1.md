# Deal & Voucher Cards — Campfire v1

10 cards for A-lite. Will expand to 20 after playtest.

## Deal cards

Deals are optional commitments. When you draw one at Crossroads, you may **accept** or **pass**.
If you accept, you commit to the Deal's terms. Completing it earns a reward. Breaking it costs Reputation.

---

### 1. Supply Run
**Terms:** Deliver 3 coins to any other player within 2 rounds.
**Reward:** +2 Reputation.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 2. Builder's Promise
**Terms:** Build 1 Upgrade within 3 rounds.
**Reward:** +1 Reputation and +1 coin.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 3. Generosity Pledge
**Terms:** Give 1 coin to each other player (this turn or next).
**Reward:** +3 Reputation.
**Penalty:** -2 Reputation if not completed.

### 4. Market Watcher
**Terms:** Buy or sell at the Market on your next 2 Market landings.
**Reward:** +1 Reputation and +2 coins.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 5. Peacekeeper
**Terms:** Help at least 1 player at Help Desk within 3 rounds.
**Reward:** +2 Reputation.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

## Voucher cards

Vouchers are player-issued IOUs. Any player can create one during a trade.
These cards represent **pre-printed voucher templates** that speed up play.

### 6. Small Loan (Voucher template)
**Face value:** 2 coins.
**Terms:** Issuer must redeem within 3 rounds when presented.
**Default penalty:** Issuer loses 2 Reputation.

### 7. Big Loan (Voucher template)
**Face value:** 4 coins.
**Terms:** Issuer must redeem within 4 rounds when presented.
**Default penalty:** Issuer loses 3 Reputation.

### 8. Favor Owed (Voucher template)
**Face value:** 1 coin + 1 Reputation transfer (issuer gives holder +1 Rep action at Help Desk for free).
**Terms:** Redeemable within 3 rounds.
**Default penalty:** Issuer loses 2 Reputation.

### 9. Trade Credit (Voucher template)
**Face value:** 3 coins, but only redeemable at a Trade Dock space.
**Terms:** Issuer must pay when holder lands on Trade Dock and presents it.
**Default penalty:** Issuer loses 2 Reputation.

### 10. Blank Voucher (Voucher template)
**Face value:** Negotiated between players (1–5 coins).
**Terms:** Negotiated deadline (1–5 rounds).
**Default penalty:** Issuer loses Reputation equal to half the face value (rounded up).

---

## Design notes

- **Deals** (1–5) are between a player and "the game" — they test commitment.
- **Vouchers** (6–10) are between players — they test trust.
- **Blank Voucher** (10) is the most powerful: fully negotiable, most like a real IOU.
- Deal penalties are mild (-1 to -2 Rep) to encourage risk-taking early.
- Voucher defaults are harsher because they betray another player, not just "the system."
