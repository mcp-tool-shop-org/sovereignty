# Deal & Voucher Cards — Campfire v1

20 cards total: 10 Deals + 10 Vouchers.

## Deal cards

Deals are optional commitments. When you draw one at Crossroads, you may **accept** or **pass**.
If you accept, you commit to the Deal's terms. Completing it earns a reward. Breaking it costs Reputation.

---

### 1. Supply Run
**Terms:** Deliver 3 coins to any other player within 2 rounds.
**Reward:** +2 Reputation.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 2. Builder's Promise
**Terms:** Build 1 Upgrade within 3 rounds.
**Reward:** +1 Reputation and +1 coin.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 3. Generosity Pledge
**Terms:** Give 1 coin to each other player (this turn or next).
**Reward:** +3 Reputation.
**Penalty:** -2 Reputation if not completed.

### 4. Market Watcher
**Terms:** Buy or sell at the Market on your next 2 Market landings.
**Reward:** +1 Reputation and +2 coins.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 5. Peacekeeper
**Terms:** Help at least 1 player at Help Desk within 3 rounds.
**Reward:** +2 Reputation.
**Penalty:** -1 Reputation if not completed.

### 6. Spot Me
**Terms:** Give 2 coins to a player now. They owe you 3 next round.
**Reward:** +3 coins, +1 Rep. **Penalty:** -1 Rep.
**Flavor:** "I'm good for it, I swear."

### 7. Two-Person Discount
**Terms:** Find a partner. You both pay 1 coin, you both gain 2.
**Reward:** +2 coins. **Penalty:** -1 Rep.
**Flavor:** "Bulk deal. Split it?"

### 8. Mutual Aid Pact
**Terms:** Pick a partner. Next time either of you hits Trouble, the other helps free.
**Reward:** +2 Rep. **Penalty:** -2 Rep.
**Flavor:** "I've got your back if you've got mine."

### 9. Reputation for Hire
**Terms:** Give 1 coin to a player. They give you +1 Rep at Help Desk.
**Reward:** +1 Rep. **Penalty:** -1 Rep.
**Flavor:** "Put in a good word for me?"

### 10. The Long Game
**Terms:** Don't spend any coins for 2 full rounds.
**Reward:** +4 coins, +1 Rep. **Penalty:** -1 Rep.
**Flavor:** "Patience pays. Eventually."

---

## Voucher cards

Vouchers are player-issued IOUs. Any player can create one during a trade.
These cards represent **pre-printed voucher templates** that speed up play.

### 11. Small Loan
**Face value:** 2 coins. **Due in:** 3 rounds.
**Default penalty:** -2 Rep.

### 12. Big Loan
**Face value:** 4 coins. **Due in:** 4 rounds.
**Default penalty:** -3 Rep.

### 13. Favor Owed
**Face value:** 1 coin + free Help Desk action. **Due in:** 3 rounds.
**Default penalty:** -2 Rep.

### 14. Trade Credit
**Face value:** 3 coins, redeemable only at Trade Dock. **Due in:** 4 rounds.
**Default penalty:** -2 Rep.

### 15. Blank Voucher
**Face value:** Negotiated (1-5 coins). **Due in:** Negotiated (1-5 rounds).
**Default penalty:** Half face value (rounded up).

### 16. Quick Cash
"I need 1 coin now. I'll pay you 2 next round. Promise."
**Face value:** 2 coins. **Due in:** 1 round. **Default penalty:** -2 Rep.

### 17. Builder's Tab
"Cover my upgrade cost (2 coins). I'll pay 3 back."
**Face value:** 3 coins. **Due in:** 3 rounds. **Default penalty:** -2 Rep.

### 18. Festival Fund
"Lend me 1 coin for Festival. I'll repay with interest."
**Face value:** 2 coins. **Due in:** 2 rounds. **Default penalty:** -1 Rep.

### 19. Emergency Loan
"I'm broke. Lend me 3 coins. I'll pay 3 back. No interest, just trust."
**Face value:** 3 coins. **Due in:** 3 rounds. **Default penalty:** -3 Rep.

### 20. Handshake Deal
"We agree on terms right now." (Negotiable, like Blank Voucher.)
**Face value:** Negotiated. **Due in:** Negotiated. **Default penalty:** Negotiated.

---

## Design notes

- **Deals** (1-10) are between a player and "the game" — they test commitment.
- **Vouchers** (11-20) are between players — they test trust.
- **Human-voice deals** (6-10) force conversation: "Hey, want to split this?" "Cover me?"
- **Human-voice vouchers** (16-20) sound like real people borrowing money.
- Deal penalties are mild (-1 to -2 Rep) to encourage risk-taking early.
- Voucher defaults are harsher because they betray another player, not just "the system."
- **Blank Voucher** and **Handshake Deal** are fully negotiable — the most like real IOUs.
