# Campfire Board — v1

16 spaces in a loop. Players start at **Campfire** (space 0).

| # | Space | Effect |
|---|-------|--------|
| 0 | **Campfire** | Safe. Collect 1 coin if you pass through (like "Go"). |
| 1 | **Workshop** | Pay 2 coins → gain 1 Upgrade. |
| 2 | **Market** | Buy or sell 1 resource at the current Market Price. |
| 3 | **Rumor Mill** | Draw an Event card and resolve it immediately. |
| 4 | **Trade Dock** | You may propose a trade with any other player (coins, vouchers, or resources). |
| 5 | **Festival** | All players may donate 1 coin to gain +1 Reputation. Optional. |
| 6 | **Trouble** | Lose 1 coin OR lose 1 Reputation. Your choice. |
| 7 | **Help Desk** | Pick another player: give them 1 coin, you both gain +1 Reputation. |
| 8 | **Mint** | Gain 2 coins from the bank. |
| 9 | **Rumor Mill** | Draw an Event card and resolve it immediately. |
| 10 | **Builder** | Pay 3 coins → gain 1 Upgrade. Requires Reputation >= 3. |
| 11 | **Faucet** | Gain 1 coin from the bank. |
| 12 | **Trade Dock** | You may propose a trade with any other player. |
| 13 | **Taxman** | Pay 1 coin to the bank. If you can't pay, lose 1 Reputation instead. |
| 14 | **Commons** | All players vote: if majority agrees, everyone gains 1 coin. Otherwise nothing. |
| 15 | **Crossroads** | Draw a Deal card. You may accept or pass. |

## Board notes

- Two **Rumor Mill** spaces (3 and 9) ensure Events come up frequently.
- Two **Trade Dock** spaces (4 and 12) encourage player interaction.
- **Workshop** (cheap upgrade) vs **Builder** (expensive upgrade + rep gate) creates a progression feel.
- **Commons** is the first taste of collective action / governance.
- **Crossroads** introduces Deals — optional commitments with future payoff.
