# Deal & Voucher Cards — Cut along the lines

## DEALS (accept or pass when drawn at Crossroads)

---

**SUPPLY RUN** | Deal
Deliver 3 coins to any player within 2 rounds.
Reward: +2 Rep | Penalty: -1 Rep

---

**BUILDER'S PROMISE** | Deal
Build 1 Upgrade within 3 rounds.
Reward: +1 Rep, +1 coin | Penalty: -1 Rep

---

**GENEROSITY PLEDGE** | Deal
Give 1 coin to each other player.
Reward: +3 Rep | Penalty: -2 Rep

---

**MARKET WATCHER** | Deal
Buy or sell at Market twice within 4 rounds.
Reward: +1 Rep, +2 coins | Penalty: -1 Rep

---

**PEACEKEEPER** | Deal
Help someone at Help Desk within 3 rounds.
Reward: +2 Rep | Penalty: -1 Rep

---

**SPOT ME** | Deal
Give 2 coins to a player now. They owe you 3 next round.
Reward: +3 coins, +1 Rep | Penalty: -1 Rep
_"I'm good for it, I swear."_

---

**TWO-PERSON DISCOUNT** | Deal
Find a partner. You both pay 1 coin, you both gain 2.
Reward: +2 coins | Penalty: -1 Rep
_"Bulk deal. Split it?"_

---

**MUTUAL AID PACT** | Deal
Pick a partner. Next Trouble, the other helps for free.
Reward: +2 Rep | Penalty: -2 Rep
_"I've got your back if you've got mine."_

---

**REPUTATION FOR HIRE** | Deal
Give 1 coin to a player. They give you +1 Rep at Help Desk.
Reward: +1 Rep | Penalty: -1 Rep
_"Put in a good word for me?"_

---

**THE LONG GAME** | Deal
Don't spend any coins for 2 full rounds.
Reward: +4 coins, +1 Rep | Penalty: -1 Rep
_"Patience pays. Eventually."_

---

## VOUCHERS (IOUs between players)

---

**SMALL LOAN** | Voucher
"I owe you 2 coins. Due in 3 rounds."
Default: -2 Rep

---

**BIG LOAN** | Voucher
"I owe you 4 coins. Due in 4 rounds."
Default: -3 Rep

---

**FAVOR OWED** | Voucher
"I owe you 1 coin and a free Help Desk. Due in 3 rounds."
Default: -2 Rep

---

**TRADE CREDIT** | Voucher
"I owe you 3 coins, but only at Trade Dock. Due in 4 rounds."
Default: -2 Rep

---

**BLANK VOUCHER** | Voucher
"We'll figure out the terms." (1-5 coins, 1-5 rounds.)
Default: half face value Rep

---

**QUICK CASH** | Voucher
"I need 1 coin now. I'll pay you 2 next round. Promise."
Default: -2 Rep

---

**BUILDER'S TAB** | Voucher
"Cover my upgrade cost (2 coins). I'll pay 3 back."
Due in 3 rounds. Default: -2 Rep

---

**FESTIVAL FUND** | Voucher
"Lend me 1 coin for Festival. I'll repay with interest."
Due in 2 rounds. Default: -1 Rep

---

**EMERGENCY LOAN** | Voucher
"I'm broke. Lend me 3 coins. No interest, just trust."
Due in 3 rounds. Default: -3 Rep

---

**HANDSHAKE DEAL** | Voucher
"We agree on terms right now." (Negotiable.)
Default: Negotiated
