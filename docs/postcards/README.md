# Postcard Gallery

After every game, `sov postcard` generates a shareable summary of what
happened. Screenshot it, paste it, talk about it.

Three styles emphasize different stories:

| Style | Command | Shows |
|-------|---------|-------|
| Cozy | `sov postcard --style cozy` | Help, apologies, toasts |
| Spicy | `sov postcard --style spicy` | Broken promises, trades, drama |
| Economic | `sov postcard --style economic` | Market mood, prices, supply |
| All | `sov postcard` | Everything |

## What makes a good postcard

A good postcard tells a story in 10 lines. The best ones have:
- A close score (tension)
- At least one promise kept or broken (character)
- A market moment or treaty event (narrative)

If your postcard tells an interesting story, open a PR to add it here.
Anonymize the player names first.
