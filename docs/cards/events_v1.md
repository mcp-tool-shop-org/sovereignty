# Event Cards — Campfire v1

20 cards. Each has: **name**, **effect**, **flavor** (one-liner on the card).

---

## Original 10

### 1. Supply Delay
**Effect:** Upgrades cost +1 coin this round (Workshop costs 3, Builder costs 4).
**Flavor:** "The shipment's late. Again."

### 2. Boom Town
**Effect:** Every player gains 1 coin from the bank.
**Flavor:** "Trade is good. Everyone's eating."

### 3. Storm
**Effect:** Every player must pay 1 coin to repairs OR lose 1 Reputation.
**Flavor:** "Batten down the hatches."

### 4. Rumor
**Effect:** You lose 1 Reputation unless another player vouches for you (they risk 1 Rep: if you lose Rep this round, they lose 1 too).
**Flavor:** "People are talking..."

### 5. Big Order
**Effect:** Market prices increase by +1 this round (selling pays more, buying costs more).
**Flavor:** "A caravan just arrived with deep pockets."

### 6. Festival of Plenty
**Effect:** The next 2 players to land on Festival gain +2 Reputation instead of +1.
**Flavor:** "The whole town is celebrating."

### 7. Swindle
**Effect:** If you hold any Vouchers from other players, you may force one redemption NOW (issuer must pay or lose 2 Rep).
**Flavor:** "Time to collect."

### 8. Windfall
**Effect:** You gain 3 coins from the bank.
**Flavor:** "Lucky day."

### 9. Drought
**Effect:** No one can buy at the Market this round.
**Flavor:** "Nothing on the shelves."

### 10. Trust Crisis
**Effect:** All players with Reputation < 3 lose 1 additional Reputation.
**Flavor:** "When trust is low, it falls further."

---

## Human-voice cards (11-20)

### 11. Lost Wallet
**Effect:** You can't trade this turn — unless someone lends you 1 coin.
**Flavor:** "Has anyone seen a small leather pouch?"

### 12. Good News Travels
**Effect:** If you helped someone last round, gain 2 coins now.
**Flavor:** "Word got around about what you did."

### 13. Awkward Favor
**Effect:** Ask any player to cover 2 coins for you. You owe them 3 later.
**Flavor:** "Hey... got a minute?"

### 14. Found a Shortcut
**Effect:** Gain 3 coins, but lose 1 Rep. People noticed.
**Flavor:** "Nobody saw... right?"

### 15. Community Dinner
**Effect:** Everyone may donate 1 coin. Each donor gains +1 Rep.
**Flavor:** "Bring something to share."

### 16. Old Friend
**Effect:** Pick a player. You each gain +1 Rep.
**Flavor:** "It's been too long."

### 17. Broken Bridge
**Effect:** Skip your next move. Stay where you are.
**Flavor:** "Road's out. Might as well make camp."

### 18. Harvest Moon
**Effect:** The player with the fewest coins gains 2 coins.
**Flavor:** "The land provides for those who need it most."

### 19. Tall Tale
**Effect:** Gain 1 Rep. But if you're already above 7, lose 1 instead.
**Flavor:** "Some stories are too good to be true."

### 20. Lucky Find
**Effect:** Draw another Event immediately.
**Flavor:** "What's this?"

---

## Design notes

- **Balance:** ~8 positive, ~6 negative, ~6 situational/social.
- **Social cards** (4, 6, 7, 13, 15, 16) require player interaction — these are the fun ones.
- **Human-voice cards** (11-20) read like moments, not mechanics. They create stories.
- **Trust Crisis** (10) punishes low-rep players, creating a "reputation floor" incentive.
- **Harvest Moon** (18) is a catch-up mechanic — helps the player who needs it most.
- **Lucky Find** (20) is recursive — draws another Event, creating surprise chains.
